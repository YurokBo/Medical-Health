SKILLED Care

Sometimes home care is not enough, and extra care is needed to maintain independence in the home and community.

Our expert care team comprised of NPs, RNs and LPNs work to enhance your quality of life and help to prevent hospital visits.

Our licensed Registered Nurses/Licensed Practical Nurses provide care in the comfort of your home.

Transfer NOW

What is Skilled Care?

Home-based services that are focused around skilled nursing, long-term nursing care, catastrophic care, tracheostomy care, ventilator care and nursing care.

How we can help

We look forward to being part of your support team during difficult times. Please give us a call and one of our dedicated professionals will be able to evaluate your unique case to give options of services that will fit your need.

Meridius Health Skilled Nursing Services include:

  • Traumatic brain injury (TBI)
  • Spinal cord injury (SCI), ALS, MS
  • Ventilator care
  • Tracheostomy care
  • Monitoring vital signs
  • Administering medications
  • Ostomy/gastrostomy care
  • Catheter care
  • Wound Care
  • Medication management and education
