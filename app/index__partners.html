What is Community Health Choices?

COMMUNITY HEALTHCHOICES (CHC) is Pennsylvania’s managed care program for individuals with physical

disabilities — serving more people in communities, giving them the opportunity to work, spend more time with their

families, and experience an overall better quality of life.

Meet the Managed Care Organizations (MCOs):

  • Keystone First https://www.keystonefirstchc.com/about/contact.aspx
  • Amerihealth Caritas https://www.amerihealthcaritaschc.com
  • PA Health & Wellness https://www.pahealthwellness.com
  • UPMC https://www.upmchealthplan.com/chc/

If you are a participant of Home and Community Based Services (HCBS), you get to be a part of a Managed Care

Organization! This is great because your care is handled by a specialized team all in one place.

Call Meridius Health today and we can help with your application for services into HCBS,

at no cost to you or your loved ones.
