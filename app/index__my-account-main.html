    • 2. Scheduling a fingerprint clearance
      • To begin, go to www.identogo.com

      • Select Pennsylvania in the drop-down menu

      • It will ask for a service code to begin registration:

        Select, Schedule or Manage an Appointment and complete all requested information/proceed with scheduling an appointment near you.
