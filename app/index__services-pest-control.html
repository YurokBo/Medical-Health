What Is Home Care?

Pest control is the regulation or management of a species defined as a pest. In homes and urban environments, pests are rodents, birds, insects and other organisms that share the habitat with humans, and that feed on and damage possessions. Control of these pests is attempted through exclusion, repulsion, physical removal or chemical means.

How we can help

Don’t let pests overrun your home! Meridius Health provides top-quality services to treat the inside of your home and develop a customized plan for protection.

  • We will not stop until your pests are gone
  • Our pest control applicators are certified with the Department of Agriculture
  • We are equipped to handle your pest problems with experience and knowledge
  • Discreet services including mice, rats, roaches, bedbugs, fleas, and other creatures that are inhibiting your safety
