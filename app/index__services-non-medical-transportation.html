What Is

Non-Medical

Transportation?

Non-medical transportation is a safe, reliable alternative for your loved ones to run errands without the hassle of riding public transportation.

How we can help

  • Our staff is trained to provide participants with safe and reliable transportation services, no matter how specific your need is.
  • Our drivers are selected for their ability to provide you maximum comfort and accommodate your physical needs.
  • Whether it is arranging a trip to the grocery store or to a church service, Meridius Health can get you where you need to go.
  • Our drivers are trusted members of the community who love what they do and strive for punctuality.
